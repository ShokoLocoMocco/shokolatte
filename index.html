Initially, my focus was Digital Marketing in my M.B.A. journey at Zicklin School of Business at Baruch College CUNY, and through Digital Marketing courseworks, I became more interested in extending my knowledge in data analysis as I started analyzing marketing data in numerical approximation.

During Covid19 pandemic that pushed us to instant uncertainty for normalcy, I decided to pursue another master's degree in Data Analysis and Visualization program at CUNY Graduate Center from Fall 2020. I am glad I made that choice, I am currently enjoying exploring new analystic models in statistical methods and visualization techniques that enable to solve real world problems here in NYC, which I believe is a great competitive advantage to lead the world of innovation and development.

Prior to receiving a college degree, I had more than ten years of extensive experience working in an international environment at top finance companies (i.e. Bloomberg, Citi, Bank of America Merrill Lynch etc.) and I consequently earned B.Sc. degree in Finance/Economics at CUNY Medgar Evers College where I successfully brought my previous experience into academic setting. My undergrad experience at Medgar devoloped the foundation of my perception not only in scientific approaches and methodologies, but also in the social contexts and values through literatures by Audre Lorde or philosophy of Angela Davis, to name a few.

I was fortunate to have been able to work in such big corporations with only high school diploma that led me to where I am today. It was made possible because in my early career, I had always loved challenges and did not hesitate to knock on the door to the new stages. The spirit lives on in me still, with much more skills added during the journey, and I am ready to contribute my experience and create something awesome with you.
